========================================
  BOMBARDIER BEETLE
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Display Name:   Beetle / Bombardier
Archetype:      UnitArchetype.BasicRanged
Role:           Ranged area-of-effect attacker. Sprays a damaging cone
                from its rear, hitting multiple enemies at once. The
                tankiest unit with the highest HP, but slower than the
                Mantis and more expensive to produce.

ECONOMY
-------
Calorie Cost:       100
Build Time:         22 seconds
Colony Capacity:    3 (supply cost per unit)
Produced At:        Underground (BuildingType.Underground)

COMBAT STATS
------------
Max HP:             42 (highest of all units)
Attack Damage:      7 (base, per target hit in cone)
Attack Range:       8.0 (ranged)
Attack Cooldown:    0.82 seconds (fastest attack speed)
Move Speed:         4.2
Vision Radius:      12
NavMesh Stop Dist:  6.8 (attackRange x 0.85)
Attack Type:        Cone spray — fires a 35-degree half-angle cone from
                    the spray origin (tail/rear). Damages ALL enemy units
                    within the cone area. Damage falls off with distance:
                    100% at origin, 60% at max range (linear interpolation).
                    Uses OverlapSphere (radius = attackRange) then filters
                    by cone angle and team.

SPRAY ATTACK DETAILS
--------------------
Cone Half-Angle:    35 degrees
Damage Falloff:     Lerp(1.0, 0.6, distance/range)
                    - Point blank: 7 damage (full)
                    - Max range (8 units): 4.2 damage (60%)
Particle Burst:     50 particles (5 bursts of 10, spaced 0.05s apart)
Particle Lifetime:  0.45 seconds (0.9x - 1.4x variation)
Particle Speed:     9 (0.9x - 1.5x variation)
Particle Cone:      26.25 degrees (visual cone = 35 x 0.75)
VFX Duration:       1.2 seconds (auto-destroy)
vs Buildings:       Does NOT use spray cone — applies direct single-target
                    damage (attackDamage) per hit, same as melee units.

SPECIAL ABILITIES
-----------------
- Area Damage:      The spray cone can hit multiple enemies simultaneously,
                    making the Bombardier effective against clustered groups.
- Rear Weapon:      The spray originates from the tail bone (or model rear).
                    During attack, the Bombardier faces AWAY from the target
                    to aim its rear weapon, unlike melee units which face
                    toward the target.
- Highest HP:       At 42 HP, can absorb more punishment than other units.
- No Gathering:     Cannot gather resources (canGather = false).

VISUAL ASPECTS
--------------
Art Source:      Meshy AI model — "Bombardier Beetle"
                Assets/_InsectWars/Units/BombardierBeetle/Art/
                Meshy_AI_Bombardier_Beetles_quadruped/
Prefab:         Assets/_InsectWars/Prefabs/BombardierBeetle.prefab
                (referenced by DefaultVisualLibrary.rangedPrefab)
Team Colors:    Neutral/white base color. Team tint applied at spawn via
                MaterialPropertyBlock (_BaseColor / _Color on all renderers).
Materials:      1-2 URP/Lit materials per model.
Spray VFX:      Procedurally generated ParticleSystem at spray origin.
                Cone-shaped emission matching the attack direction.

ANIMATIONS
----------
Idle:       Default standing pose. The unit plays a looping idle animation
            when stationary and not performing any action.

Walk:       Quadruped locomotion cycle. Embedded in the source FBX as
            "Walking_withSkin". Driven by the "Speed" (float) and
            "IsMoving" (bool) animator parameters. Blends based on the
            unit's current planar velocity.

Attack:     Triggered via the "Attack" animator trigger. Spray attack
            duration is 0.50 seconds (longer than melee's 0.35s). The
            Bombardier rotates to face AWAY from the target, aiming its
            rear toward the enemy. The spray origin is determined by
            finding the tail bone; if no tail bone exists, it falls back
            to an offset behind the model root. A burst of 50 particles
            fires in a cone while area damage is applied.

Death:      Triggered via the "Death" animator trigger when the unit
            is killed. Plays a collapse/fall animation before the
            GameObject is destroyed.

Animator Controller: Assets/_InsectWars/Animations/BombardierBeetle.controller
States: Idle, Walk (Attack/Death driven by parameters/triggers)
