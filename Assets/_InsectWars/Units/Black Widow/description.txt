========================================
  BLACK WIDOW
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Display Name:   Black Widow
Archetype:      UnitArchetype.BlackWidow
Role:           Elite melee assassin with debuff abilities. Delivers
                venomous bites that poison enemies over time and
                periodically casts a web net that slows groups of
                enemies. Expensive but devastating in prolonged fights.

ECONOMY
-------
Calorie Cost:       250
Build Time:         35 seconds
Colony Capacity:    5 (supply cost per unit)
Produced At:        Underground (BuildingType.Underground)

COMBAT STATS
------------
Max HP:             55 (tankiest unit in the game)
Attack Damage:      6 (direct melee damage per hit)
Attack Range:       1.55 (melee)
Attack Cooldown:    1.1 seconds
Move Speed:         4.8
Vision Radius:      12
Attack Type:        Melee — locks NavMesh agent position during attack,
                    faces target, applies single-target damage per hit.
                    Each hit also applies a poison debuff to the target.

POISON BITE (passive, on every melee hit)
-----------------------------------------
Damage Per Second:  2.0
Duration:           15 seconds
Total Poison Dmg:   30 (over full duration)
Stacking:           Does NOT stack — re-hitting refreshes the duration.
                    Only one poison instance per target at a time.
Application:        Automatically applied via PoisonDebuff component
                    added to the target on melee hit.
Visual:             Green-tinted damage ticks on the poisoned unit.

WEB NET (auto-cast ability, every 10 seconds)
----------------------------------------------
Cooldown:           10 seconds
Range:              5 units (short cone in front)
Cone Half-Angle:    45 degrees
Slow Amount:        50% movement speed reduction
Slow Duration:      8 seconds
Stacking:           Does NOT stack — re-application refreshes duration.
Targeting:          Auto-casts when enemies are in the forward cone.
                    Only fires during combat (unit must have an attack
                    target). Fires automatically, not player-triggered.
Visual:             Web/silk particle burst in a cone area.
Implementation:     WebNetAbility component on the Black Widow unit.
                    Applies a speed multiplier debuff tracked on each
                    affected InsectUnit.

SPECIAL ABILITIES
-----------------
- Poison Bite:      Every melee attack applies 2 DPS poison for 15s.
                    Combined with 6 direct damage per 1.1s cooldown,
                    effective DPS is ~7.45 (direct) + 2 (poison) while
                    poison is active.
- Web Net:          Auto-cast AoE slow — devastating against groups.
                    Slowed enemies can't escape or kite effectively.
- Highest HP:       At 55 HP, the tankiest unit in the game.
- Premium Cost:     At 250 calories and 5 CC, she's a late-game
                    investment that pays off in extended engagements.
- No Gathering:     Cannot gather resources (canGather = false).

ANIMATIONS
----------
Idle:       Default standing pose. The unit plays a looping idle
            animation when stationary. Subtle body sway and leg
            adjustments conveying a predatory readiness.

Walk:       Eight-legged locomotion cycle. Smooth, deliberate spider
            gait. Driven by the "Speed" (float) and "IsMoving" (bool)
            animator parameters. Blends based on planar velocity.

Attack:     Triggered via the "Attack" animator trigger. Melee bite
            duration is 0.40 seconds (slightly longer than Mantis to
            convey the venomous bite). The Black Widow lunges forward,
            fangs strike the target. Faces toward target like other
            melee units.

WebCast:    Triggered via the "WebCast" animator trigger. The Black
            Widow rears back slightly and projects silk forward in a
            short cone. Duration ~0.5 seconds. Plays independently
            of the attack cycle (auto-cast on cooldown).

Death:      Triggered via the "Death" animator trigger when the unit
            is killed. Legs curl inward as the body collapses — the
            classic spider death pose.

Animator Controller: Assets/_InsectWars/Animations/BlackWidow.controller
States: Idle, Walk (Attack/WebCast/Death driven by parameters/triggers)
