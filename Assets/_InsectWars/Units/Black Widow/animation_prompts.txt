========================================
  BLACK WIDOW — UNITY AI ANIMATION PROMPTS
========================================

Model: Meshy AI "Crimson Widow" — a dark, realistic black widow spider
       with eight legs, compact thorax, bulbous abdomen, and prominent fangs.
       The model is oriented with -Z forward (rotated -90 X in the prefab).

Target: Unity 6 Muse Animate or equivalent AI animation tool.
        Each animation should be exported as a separate .anim clip and
        referenced by the BlackWidow.controller animator.

Animator Parameters:
  - Speed     (float)  — planar velocity magnitude
  - IsMoving  (bool)   — true when speed > 0.1
  - Attack    (trigger) — melee bite
  - WebCast   (trigger) — web net ability
  - Death     (trigger) — unit killed


─────────────────────────────────────
1. IDLE  (looping)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider standing still in a neutral idle pose. Subtle,
   looping body sway — the abdomen gently bobs up and down by a few
   millimeters. The front pair of legs occasionally lift and reposition
   slightly, as if feeling the ground. All eight legs planted with a
   wide, stable stance. The cephalothorax tilts forward very slightly
   in a predatory ready posture. Slow, breathing-like rhythm. Loop
   duration approximately 2-3 seconds."

Clip name: BlackWidow_Idle
Loop: Yes
Duration: ~2.5 seconds


─────────────────────────────────────
2. WALK  (looping)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider walking forward with a smooth, deliberate
   eight-legged gait. Legs move in an alternating tetrapod pattern —
   four legs lift while the other four stay grounded, then they swap.
   The body stays low and level, gliding forward with minimal vertical
   bounce. The abdomen sways slightly side to side with each step
   cycle. Movement feels purposeful and predatory, not frantic.
   Medium walking speed. Loop duration approximately 1 second for
   one full step cycle."

Clip name: BlackWidow_Walk
Loop: Yes
Duration: ~1.0 second (one full step cycle)
Speed parameter: Playback rate scaled by the Speed animator float.


─────────────────────────────────────
3. ATTACK — Melee Bite  (one-shot)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider performing a fast, venomous melee bite attack.
   The spider lunges its cephalothorax forward and downward. The front
   two legs spread apart to grab the target. The fangs (chelicerae)
   open wide and snap shut in a piercing bite motion. The body recoils
   back to its original position after the strike. The movement should
   feel sharp and aggressive — a quick lunge-bite-retract sequence.
   Total duration approximately 0.4 seconds. The abdomen stays mostly
   stable during the lunge."

Clip name: BlackWidow_Attack
Loop: No
Duration: ~0.40 seconds
Trigger: "Attack" animator trigger
Notes: Plays each time the unit deals melee damage (every 1.1s cooldown).
       The unit is position-locked and facing the target during this clip.


─────────────────────────────────────
4. WEB CAST — Web Net Ability  (one-shot)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider casting a web net forward. The spider rears its
   front body upward slightly — the first two pairs of legs lift off
   the ground while the rear four legs anchor it. The abdomen tilts
   forward and upward, pointing the spinnerets toward the front. A
   quick pulsing motion of the abdomen suggests silk being expelled
   (the actual particle VFX is handled separately). The front legs
   then sweep outward in a spreading gesture, as if directing the
   web in a cone shape. The spider then settles back to its idle
   stance. Total duration approximately 0.5 seconds."

Clip name: BlackWidow_WebCast
Loop: No
Duration: ~0.50 seconds
Trigger: "WebCast" animator trigger
Notes: Auto-cast every 10 seconds during combat. Plays independently
       from the melee attack cycle. A particle VFX cone is spawned
       at the same time by the WebNetAbility component.


─────────────────────────────────────
5. DEATH  (one-shot)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider dying. The spider's legs buckle and curl inward
   toward the body — the classic real-world spider death curl. The body
   tips slightly to one side and collapses to the ground. All eight
   legs fold underneath and contract. The abdomen settles flat on the
   ground. The movement should feel like the life draining out — not
   an explosion or violent ragdoll, but a gradual curling collapse.
   Total duration approximately 0.5 seconds. The final pose holds
   with all legs curled tightly against the body."

Clip name: BlackWidow_Death
Loop: No
Duration: ~0.50 seconds
Trigger: "Death" animator trigger
Notes: After the clip ends, the GameObject is destroyed after a short delay.
       The final curled pose should be the last keyframe (hold pose).


─────────────────────────────────────
6. POISON HIT REACTION (optional, one-shot)
─────────────────────────────────────
Prompt:
  "A black widow spider performing a subtle hit reaction, as if
   something stung it. A quick flinch — the body jerks slightly
   backward, two front legs raise defensively, then immediately
   return to normal stance. Very fast, almost a twitch. This plays
   on the TARGET unit being poisoned, not on the Black Widow itself.
   Duration approximately 0.15 seconds."

Clip name: BlackWidow_HitReact (or shared HitReact clip)
Loop: No
Duration: ~0.15 seconds
Notes: Optional. This would play on any unit receiving poison damage ticks.
       Can be shared across all unit types, not specific to Black Widow.


========================================
  ANIMATOR CONTROLLER STRUCTURE
========================================

Controller: Assets/_InsectWars/Animations/BlackWidow.controller

States:
  - Idle      (default state, plays BlackWidow_Idle, looping)
  - Walk      (plays BlackWidow_Walk, looping)
  - Attack    (plays BlackWidow_Attack, returns to Idle/Walk)
  - WebCast   (plays BlackWidow_WebCast, returns to Idle/Walk)
  - Death     (plays BlackWidow_Death, no exit)

Transitions:
  Idle -> Walk:     IsMoving == true
  Walk -> Idle:     IsMoving == false
  Any  -> Attack:   Attack trigger
  Any  -> WebCast:  WebCast trigger
  Any  -> Death:    Death trigger (no exit transition)
  Attack -> Idle:   Has Exit Time (clip end)
  WebCast -> Idle:  Has Exit Time (clip end)
