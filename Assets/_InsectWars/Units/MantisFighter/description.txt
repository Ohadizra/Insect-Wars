========================================
  MANTIS FIGHTER
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Display Name:   Mantis
Archetype:      UnitArchetype.BasicFighter
Role:           Fast melee assault unit. Closes distance quickly and
                locks onto enemies in close combat. The primary frontline
                fighter of the colony.

ECONOMY
-------
Calorie Cost:       100
Build Time:         18 seconds
Colony Capacity:    2 (supply cost per unit)
Produced At:        Underground (BuildingType.Underground)

COMBAT STATS
------------
Max HP:             38
Attack Damage:      9
Attack Range:       1.55 (melee)
Attack Cooldown:    0.95 seconds
Move Speed:         5.4 (fastest unit)
Vision Radius:      12
Attack Type:        Melee — locks NavMesh agent position during attack
                    (agent.updatePosition = false). Faces target and
                    applies full damage to a single enemy per hit.
                    Unlocks movement when target moves beyond leash
                    distance (range x 1.25).

SPECIAL ABILITIES
-----------------
- Melee Lock:       When attacking, the Mantis stops its NavMesh agent
                    and locks in place to deliver consistent melee hits.
                    This prevents jittering during close-range combat.
- High Speed:       At 5.4 move speed, the Mantis is the fastest unit
                    in the game, able to chase down fleeing enemies or
                    rapidly reposition.
- No Gathering:     Cannot gather resources (canGather = false).

VISUAL ASPECTS
--------------
Art Source:      Meshy AI model — "Ironclad Mantis Automaton"
                Assets/_InsectWars/Units/MantisFighter/Art/
                Meshy_AI_Ironclad_Mantis_Autom_quadruped/
Prefab:         Assets/_InsectWars/Prefabs/MantisFighter.prefab
                (referenced by DefaultVisualLibrary.meleePrefab)
Team Colors:    Neutral/white base color. Team tint applied at spawn via
                MaterialPropertyBlock (_BaseColor / _Color on all renderers).
Materials:      1-2 URP/Lit materials per model.

ANIMATIONS
----------
Idle:       Default standing pose with an extended procedural idle loop.
            The Mantis has a longer procedural idle animation compared
            to other units, giving it a distinctive resting posture
            when stationary and not in combat.

Walk:       Quadruped locomotion cycle. Embedded in the source FBX as
            "Walking_withSkin". Driven by the "Speed" (float) and
            "IsMoving" (bool) animator parameters. Blends based on the
            unit's current planar velocity. At 5.4 speed, the walk
            cycle plays noticeably faster than other units.

Attack:     Triggered via the "Attack" animator trigger. Melee attack
            duration is 0.35 seconds. The Mantis faces its target and
            performs a rapid striking slash. Damage (9) is applied once
            per cooldown cycle (0.95s). During attack, the unit locks
            its position and rotates to face the target.

Death:      Triggered via the "Death" animator trigger when the unit
            is killed. Plays a collapse/fall animation before the
            GameObject is destroyed.

Animator Controller: Assets/_InsectWars/Animations/MantisFighter.controller
States: Idle, Walk (Attack/Death driven by parameters/triggers)
