========================================
  ANT WORKER
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Display Name:   Worker
Archetype:      UnitArchetype.Worker
Role:           Economic backbone of the colony. Gathers calories from
                rotting fruit nodes, deposits them at the hive or ant nests,
                and constructs new buildings. Weak in combat.

ECONOMY
-------
Calorie Cost:       50
Build Time:         10 seconds
Colony Capacity:    1 (supply cost per unit)
Produced At:        Hive (HiveDeposit) or Ant Nest (BuildingType.AntNest)

COMBAT STATS
------------
Max HP:             35
Attack Damage:      4
Attack Range:       1.55 (melee)
Attack Cooldown:    0.95 seconds
Move Speed:         4.0
Vision Radius:      12
Attack Type:        Melee — locks NavMesh agent position during attack,
                    faces target, applies single-target damage per hit.
                    Weakest combat unit; should avoid fights.

SPECIAL ABILITIES
-----------------
- Gathering:    Harvests 10 calories per tick (every 5 seconds) from
                RottingFruitNode. Must be within gather range (fruit visual
                radius + 1.5 units). Cargo stored in WorkerInventory,
                then deposited at hive or nearest operational ant nest.
- Auto-gather:  Idle workers scan every 0.5s for fruit within 30 units.
                After completing a move order, workers scan within 6 units
                and auto-gather if fruit is nearby.
- Construction: Can be ordered to build structures. Walks to build site
                and ticks construction progress on the target building.
- Deposit:      Returns gathered calories to the nearest valid deposit
                point (player hive or operational AntNest).

ANIMATIONS
----------
Idle:       Default standing pose. The unit plays a looping idle animation
            when stationary and not performing any action.

Walk:       Quadruped locomotion cycle. Embedded in the source FBX as
            "Walking_withSkin". Driven by the "Speed" (float) and
            "IsMoving" (bool) animator parameters. Blends based on the
            unit's current planar velocity.

Attack:     Triggered via the "Attack" animator trigger. Melee attack
            duration is 0.35 seconds. The unit faces the target and
            performs a short striking motion. Damage is applied once
            per cooldown cycle (0.95s).

Gathering:  Controlled by the "Gathering" (bool) animator parameter.
            Set to true when the worker is actively harvesting at a
            fruit node and the NavMesh agent has stopped. The worker
            performs a repetitive harvesting motion at the fruit.

Build:      Intended "Build" (bool) animator parameter. Activated when
            the worker is constructing a building. Currently defined in
            UnitAnimationDriver but not yet wired into the animator
            controller states.

Death:      Triggered via the "Death" animator trigger when the unit
            is killed. Plays a collapse/fall animation before the
            GameObject is destroyed.

Animator Controller: Assets/_InsectWars/Animations/AntWorker.controller
States: Idle, Walk (Attack/Gathering/Death driven by parameters/triggers)
